Arjuna Visada Yoga

The first chapter of the Bhagavad Gita - "Arjuna Vishada Yoga" introduces the setup, the setting, the characters and the circumstances that led to the epic battle of Mahabharata, fought between the Pandavas and the Kauravas. It outlines the reasons that led to the revelation of the Gita.

Chapter 2
Sankhya Yoga

The second chapter of the Bhagavad Gita - "Sankhya Yoga" deals with the concepts of knowledge and wisdom. It emphasizes the importance of understanding the self and the distinction between the physical body and the eternal soul. This chapter lays the foundation for the teachings that follow.

Chapter 3
Karma Yoga

The third chapter of the Bhagavad Gita - "Karma Yoga" focuses on the concept of selfless action and the importance of performing one's duty without attachment to the results. It teaches that one should act according to their dharma (duty) and offer the fruits of their actions to the divine.
